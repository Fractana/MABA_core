MABA_core

Symbolic-operational core powering Fractana

Mission: Empower communities through modular, replicable units for innovation in education, energy, and AI.

What is Fractana?

Fractana is a generative framework developed by MABA to activate local creativity, sustainable production, and intelligent learning. Each deployment is context-adaptive, replicable, and rooted in environmental and social justice.

Core Modules

  • modules/ – Customizable operational units
  • ai_models/ – Applied AI for diagnostics and learning
  • docs/ – Symbolic and institutional documentation
  • interfaces/ – APIs and UI components
